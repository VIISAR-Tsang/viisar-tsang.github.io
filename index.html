The VIISAR research group (Visão e Inteligência Artificial, in portuguese) is part of the Center of Informatics (CIn) at the Federal University of Pernambuco (UFPE). The principal research areas are Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Pattern Recognition, Image Processing, Document Analysis and Recognition, Biometry.
